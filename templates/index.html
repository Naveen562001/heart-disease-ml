  • atypical angina - back pain or pain that is described as burning, stabbing
  • non anginal pain - heart pain after a reasonable workup has excluded a cardiac cause
  • Asysmtomatic - a transient alteration in myocardial perfusion in the absence of chest pain

  • flat,Downsloping - reduced blood flow to heart
